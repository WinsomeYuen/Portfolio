Surplus

This was a 3 month project created for my software engineering module at university working in a group of 5. The website's aim was to reduce food waste caused by restaurants and supermarkets, by offering a platform that would benefit both the customers and businesses, by creating a platform which allowed businesses to sell products that were close to expiration, undesirable, over-stocked for a cheaper price to customers. To do this the web application would store products for each businesses in a database, that would be retrieved when customers clicked on a location marker on a interactive map to lok at the products available. Customers can add products from different businesses and the Stripe API is used to process payments. Past orders would be stored for each customer in a database that stored their logins.


I was the Project leader for the group which consisted of:
  • Finalising the designs and requirements for the application
  • Ensuring all requirements were met during development
  • Taking on a full-stack developer role
  • Keeping track of team member's progress and assisting if help was required
  • Deciding on the technology to use on the project based on team members skills and opinions


Research

Once we brainstormed some ideas and picked the food waste topic as the idea for our project, we did deeper research on the topic. We found some similar applications, but they were all mobile apps, which everyone may not have a smartphone to access the app. We found some interesting facts, that we featured in our research and was the basis for designing our website.
