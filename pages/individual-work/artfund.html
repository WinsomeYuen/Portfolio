Improvements for the future

This art competition piece really inspired me to go into design as it had been a long time since I got excited about working on a project not for university or work. There were a lot of things I learnt from thsi experience too:

  1. Concentrate on a specific idea. I had tried to include so many different ideas into one piece that it got a bit cluttered and made it difficult for an audience to grab the idea of what the posters ar presenting
  2. The main text I want to highlight should be more centered. I had placed the price and the card itself right at the bottom, which can be easily missed by somebody looking at the poster.
  3. Research more on the interests of the target audience. Although I felt the design stood out cause of the style and colours used, it would be mistaken to be aimed at children because of the imagery used.

Other Designs I had created

These were the other designs I had created before the final design above. Looking back on them now and reflecting on what the competition brief was, I think some of these designs would of worked better as an entry. So note to self for the future is that less is more! I don't need to do some fancy intricate design to get the attention of the audience. I just need something easy to see in a crowd and communicates the message directly.
